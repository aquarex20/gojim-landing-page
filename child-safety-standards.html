Child safety standards
This page describes GoJim’s standards and enforcement approach against child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and child sexual exploitation and abuse (CSAE).
Last updated: April 1, 2026 Contact: gymbrahapp@gmail.com

Zero tolerance

GoJim has zero tolerance for CSAM/CSAE. We prohibit any content, behavior, or attempt to exploit minors.

  • No sharing, requesting, or grooming-related behavior.
  • No accounts used to target or contact minors for exploitation.

Enforcement

When we become aware of violations, we take action which may include removing content, restricting features, and disabling accounts.

  • Account suspension/termination for severe or repeated violations.
  • Cooperation with lawful requests from authorities when applicable.
Report / Contact: If you believe a user is engaging in harmful behavior involving minors, contact us at gymbrahapp@gmail.com.
